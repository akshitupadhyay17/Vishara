Common Questions

Design protection covers the ornamental appearance of manufactured articles, including shape, configuration, surface ornamentation, patterns, and GUI elements.
Design protection covers appearance/aesthetics while utility patents cover function. Many products benefit from both types of protection.
In the U.S., design patents last 15 years from grant. International registered designs vary by jurisdiction, typically 10-25 years with renewals.
