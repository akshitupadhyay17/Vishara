Design Services

User Research & Discovery

Stakeholder interviews, user persona development, journey mapping, competitive UX audits, and usability testing to uncover pain points, behavioral patterns, and opportunities that inform every design decision.

Wireframing & Information Architecture

Low-fidelity wireframes and site maps that establish content hierarchy, navigation flows, and page layouts — creating a blueprint for user experience before any visual design begins.

Visual Design & Prototyping

High-fidelity mockups in Figma with pixel-perfect precision, interactive prototypes for stakeholder review and usability testing, micro-animations, and responsive design variants for all breakpoints.

Design Systems & Component Libraries

Scalable design systems with reusable UI components, typography scales, color palettes, spacing tokens, and interaction patterns — ensuring visual consistency across your entire product ecosystem.

Mobile App Design

Native iOS and Android app design following platform-specific guidelines (Human Interface Guidelines, Material Design), with gesture-based interactions, offline-first considerations, and app store asset creation.

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

Data-driven UX improvements through heatmap analysis, session recordings, A/B testing, and funnel optimization — transforming user insights into measurable conversion lifts across your digital touchpoints.

UX (User Experience) design focuses on the overall feel, flow, and usability of a product — how easy and satisfying it is to use. UI (User Interface) design focuses on the visual appearance — colors, typography, buttons, and layouts. We handle both to deliver cohesive digital experiences.
Our primary tool is Figma for design and prototyping, supported by FigJam for collaborative workshops, Maze for usability testing, Hotjar for behavioral analytics, and Lottie for micro-animation production. All deliverables are Figma-native for seamless developer handoff.
Our standard process includes 2–3 concept directions, followed by 2 rounds of revisions on the selected direction. Most projects reach final approval within these iterations. Additional rounds can be added for complex projects at a transparent per-round rate.
