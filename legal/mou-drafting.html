What We Offer

MOU Services

Partnership MOUs

Pre-contractual frameworks for business partnerships defining scope of collaboration, resource commitments, revenue sharing principles, governance structures, and timelines for transitioning to a formal agreement.

Joint Venture MOUs

Foundation documents for joint ventures establishing equity splits, capital contributions, management responsibilities, IP ownership, profit distribution, exit mechanisms, and deadlock resolution procedures.

Government & Institutional MOUs

Inter-agency and public-private MOUs following governmental protocol requirements, including compliance with procurement regulations, sovereign immunity considerations, and public accountability standards.

International MOUs

Cross-border cooperation agreements addressing language requirements, governing law selection, currency provisions, cultural protocols, and alignment with international trade frameworks and bilateral treaties.

Research Collaboration MOUs

Academic and corporate research MOUs defining study objectives, data sharing protocols, publication rights, IP ownership, funding arrangements, and ethical review requirements.

MOU-to-Contract Transition

Guided conversion of MOUs into binding definitive agreements, ensuring all preliminary understandings are accurately captured, expanded with necessary legal detail, and commercially enforceable.
