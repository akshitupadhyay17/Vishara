What We Offer

Franchise Legal Services

Franchise Agreement Drafting

Comprehensive franchise agreements covering territorial rights, fee structures (initial franchise fees, ongoing royalties, advertising fund contributions), operating standards, quality control provisions, and renewal/termination terms.

Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD)

Preparation of all 23 FDD items required by the FTC Franchise Rule, including franchisor financial statements, litigation history, initial investment estimates, and franchisee obligations — fully compliant with state registration requirements.

Area Development Agreements

Multi-unit development deals that grant franchisees exclusive rights to open a specified number of units within a defined territory and timeline, with development schedules and performance benchmarks.

Master Franchise & Sub-Franchise

International expansion structures where master franchisees recruit and manage sub-franchisees in foreign markets, handling local regulatory compliance, currency considerations, and cultural adaptations.

Brand Protection & IP Licensing

Trademark licensing provisions within franchise agreements that maintain brand consistency, along with trade dress protection, proprietary system safeguards, and non-compete enforcement strategies.

Franchise Dispute Resolution

Mediation, arbitration, and litigation support for franchise relationship disputes including termination disagreements, encroachment claims, earnings representation issues, and franchisee non-compliance.
