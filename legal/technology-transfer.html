What We Offer

Technology Transfer Services

University-Industry Transfer

Structuring agreements between academic institutions and commercial enterprises for sponsored research, IP ownership allocation, royalty sharing, and publication rights that satisfy both academic freedom and commercial interests.

International Technology Transfer

Cross-border technology transfer agreements that address export control compliance (ITAR/EAR), foreign investment restrictions, local content requirements, and multi-jurisdictional IP protection strategies.

Joint Development Agreements (JDA)

Collaborative R&D contracts defining each party's contributions, background IP, foreground IP ownership, cost sharing, milestone payments, and commercialization rights for jointly developed technologies.

Know-How & Trade Secret Transfer

Structured transfer of unpatented technical knowledge, manufacturing processes, formulations, and operational know-how — including training obligations, technical assistance periods, and confidentiality safeguards.

Government & Defense Tech Transfer

Specialized agreements for government-funded technology commercialization, including Bayh-Dole Act compliance, CRADA structures, march-in rights provisions, and security-classified IP handling.

Technology Valuation

Independent assessment of technology value using cost-based, market-based, and income-based approaches to establish fair transfer pricing for licensing, sale, or partnership negotiations.

Common Questions

A typical agreement covers the scope of technology being transferred, IP ownership rights, payment terms, technical assistance and training, warranties, indemnities, confidentiality obligations, improvement ownership, and termination provisions.
We carefully define "background IP" (pre-existing) and "foreground IP" (newly created). Foreground IP can be owned jointly, by one party with a license back, or divided by field of use — depending on each party's contributions and commercial objectives.
Depending on the technology, ITAR (defense articles), EAR (dual-use items), or EU dual-use regulations may apply. We conduct export classification reviews and build compliance obligations directly into the transfer agreement to prevent violations.
