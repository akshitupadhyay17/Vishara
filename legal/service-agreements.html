Service Agreements

Protect your business relationships with clear, comprehensive service agreements that define scope, deliverables, payments, and liability.

What We Offer

Service Agreement Solutions

Master Service Agreements (MSA)

Comprehensive framework agreements that govern long-term service relationships, with modular Statements of Work (SOWs) for individual projects — reducing negotiation time and providing consistent terms across engagements.

Service Level Agreements (SLA)

Measurable performance standards, uptime guarantees, response time commitments, and penalty/credit mechanisms that hold service providers accountable to agreed quality benchmarks.

Consulting & Advisory Agreements

Contracts for professional consultants covering scope of advisory services, deliverables, hourly/fixed-fee structures, IP ownership of work product, non-solicitation, and conflict of interest provisions.

IT & Software Services Contracts

Agreements for software development, implementation, support, and maintenance — including acceptance criteria, warranty periods, escrow arrangements, data handling obligations, and change order processes.

Outsourcing Agreements

End-to-end outsourcing contracts for business process outsourcing (BPO), IT outsourcing (ITO), and knowledge process outsourcing (KPO) — with transition planning, governance frameworks, and exit strategies.

Maintenance & Support Agreements

Post-sale service contracts covering preventive maintenance schedules, break-fix response times, spare parts provisioning, software update entitlements, and extended warranty terms.
