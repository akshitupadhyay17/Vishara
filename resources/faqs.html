General Questions

Working with VISHARA IP

How do you price your services?

For legal and IP services, we typically offer flat-fee pricing for standard filings and drafting to provide predictability. For complex litigation or bespoke consulting, we use transparent hourly rates. Marketing services are usually structured as monthly retainers.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. We represent global clients seeking IP protection in the US and collaborate with a network of trusted foreign associates to secure international rights via PCT, Madrid Protocol, and direct foreign filings.

What is your typical onboarding process?

Our process starts with a free initial discovery call. Upon engagement, we hold a kickoff meeting to define scope, establish communication channels, and collect necessary documentation before executing the agreed strategy.

Do you offer free consultations?

We offer a complimentary 30-minute introductory call to discuss your needs, assess if we are a good fit, and outline potential strategies. In-depth legal analysis or strategy formulation requires a formal engagement.

Who will be managing my project?

You will be assigned a dedicated project manager or lead attorney who acts as your primary point of contact, ensuring seamless communication and coordination across our legal and digital teams.

Why combine legal and marketing services?

Modern businesses need cohesive strategies. By integrating IP protection with brand marketing, we ensure that the brand identity you are promoting is legally secured, preventing costly rebranding or infringement issues down the line.
